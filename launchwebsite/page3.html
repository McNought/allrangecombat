Operations Captain

Duties and responsibilities

The Operations Captain is under the direct supervison of the Assistant Superintendent of Security and is responsible for the physical, operational, security surveillance, and supervison of the assigned work sections.

The Operations Captain is familiar with the DOC rules, policies, OSP procedures, post orders, and directives that affect each assigned work section's safety, security and orderly operation as follows:

  • Oregon Corrections Enterprise (OCE)
  • Industries shop areas
  • Physical Plant
  • Vocational Training, Automotive
  • Security Gate
  • 2 and 5 vehicle gates
  • Master Control
  • Tool and Key Sergeant
  • OSP/MCCF Tool and Key Control
  • ...and other areas or work sections as assigned
