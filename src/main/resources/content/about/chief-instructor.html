Classes in Leighton Buzzard are taught by Chief Instructor Colin Lawrence who has trained since 1975 and has been taught and graded by the first generation instructors in this country and the USA.

Colin Lawrence is a 3rd Dan Black Belt in Tae-Kwon-Do and has been practising Taekwondo/Thai boxing/Jiu Jitsu and Kali for 39 years.
