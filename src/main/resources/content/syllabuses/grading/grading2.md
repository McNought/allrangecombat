Required for Orange belt:

- Everything on Yellow Belt Syllabus
- Fighting stance. 
- Footwork
	- forward-back-side-side
- Boxing Combos
	- Jab/double
	- jab/cross/hook/uppercut
- Kicks
	- Front-kick
	- Round-kick
	- Side-kick
- Releases
	- grabs to neck one and two hands.
- Patterns one and two
- 15-30 press-ups  
- 20-30 sit-ups
